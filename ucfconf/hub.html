üìã How to Create Your AI Agent

Follow these simple steps to create a custom AI tutoring agent for your course:

1

Complete Form

Fill out our comprehensive form with your course details, learning objectives, and teaching preferences. The more specific you are, the better your AI agent will be.

2

Customize Form with Specifics

Add detailed information about your course content, student needs, and preferred teaching approach. Include example interactions and specify what the agent should and shouldn't do.

3

Choose How to Obtain Prompt

Select whether to download the prompt as a text file or have it emailed to you. You can also preview it first to make sure everything looks correct.

4

Copy Prompt for Agent Creation

Take the generated prompt and paste it into Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini. The AI will create detailed, ready-to-use instructions for your Blackbox agent.

5

Paste Into Your Chatbot of Choice

Use Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini to process your prompt. The AI will generate comprehensive instructions specifically designed for effective tutoring.

6

Navigate to Blackbox AI

Go to blackbox.ai and click "Create Agent" in the sidebar to start building your custom tutoring assistant.

7

Create Agent with Name, Image, and Instructions

Give your agent a name, generate or upload a profile image, and paste the custom instructions from step 5 into the instructions field. Click "Create Agent" to finish.
