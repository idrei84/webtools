AI Priority Areas: Quick Examples

The first step in your AI integration journey is identifying which priority area to focus on. Here are brief examples for each area:

Instruction & Curriculum

  • AI-enhanced adaptive course materials
  • Intelligent tutoring systems
  • "AI-resilient" assignment design

Student Services

  • Virtual AI advisors for enrollment
  • Predictive analytics for student success
  • 24/7 chatbots for common questions

Administrative Operations

  • AI-powered scheduling optimization
  • Automated document processing
  • Smart facilities management

Institutional Research

  • Data analytics for student success patterns
  • Automated reporting for accreditation
  • AI-enhanced program review

Professional Development

  • Personalized AI learning platforms
  • Virtual reality training simulations
  • AI coaching for teaching improvement

Other Potential Areas

  • Workforce Development: AI labor market analysis
  • Accessibility Services: AI for content adaptation
  • Adult Education: Personalized learning paths
